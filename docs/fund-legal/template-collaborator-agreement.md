## 2. Compensation

**2.1 No fixed salary.** Collaborator receives no fixed salary or guaranteed compensation under this Agreement.

**2.2 Revenue participation.** Upon the [PROJECT NAME] project reaching its defined runway threshold (as described in the Lab's Fund and Disbursement Model, incorporated by reference), Collaborator participates in the project's time collaborator pool based on their Tier weight:

| Tier | Weight |
|---|---|
| Core Collaborator | 1.0x |
| Contributing Collaborator | 0.6x |
| Advisor | 0.25x |

Collaborator's Tier weight for this Agreement is: **[WEIGHT]x**

**2.3 Pool mechanics.** The time collaborator pool receives approximately 30% of the project's 85% revenue share. Collaborator's share within the pool is proportional to their weight relative to the total weighted points of all active time collaborators in the same quarter.

**2.4 Year-1 window.** The year-1 disbursement window opens upon the project's first personnel disbursement and runs for 12 rolling months. Full terms are in the Fund and Disbursement Model.

**2.5 No runway, no disbursement.** If the project does not reach its runway threshold, no disbursements are made and no compensation is owed to Collaborator under this Agreement.

**2.6 Core pool (lab-wide roles only).** If Collaborator holds a lab-wide role (Executive Director, Director of Strategy and Partnerships, Lab Manager, Legal, HR, Sales), compensation is instead earned through the core pool points system as described in the Fund and Disbursement Model. ## 3. Intellectual Property

**3.1 Assignment.** Collaborator assigns to the Lab, effective as of the date each work product is created, all right, title, and interest in any intellectual property created by Collaborator in the course of their engagement under this Agreement, including but not limited to: research findings, instrument designs, measurement methodologies, software, documentation, creative works, and any other work product. This assignment is worldwide, perpetual, and irrevocable.

**3.2 Pre-existing IP.** Collaborator retains ownership of any intellectual property created prior to this Agreement ("Pre-existing IP"). If Collaborator incorporates Pre-existing IP into work product produced under this Agreement, Collaborator grants the Lab a perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free license to use such Pre-existing IP as part of the work product.

**3.3 Moral rights.** To the extent permitted by applicable law, Collaborator waives any moral rights or rights of attribution in work product created under this Agreement.

**3.4 Cooperation.** Collaborator agrees to execute any documents and take any further actions reasonably requested by the Lab to perfect or record the Lab's rights in work product.

## 4. Vesting and Performance Review

**4.1 Vesting schedule.** Collaborator's revenue participation rights vest on a quarterly basis from the Effective Date of this Agreement. Rights vest only for quarters in which Collaborator has been active and met the performance standards described below.

**4.2 Quarterly review.** The Executive Director conducts a quarterly review of all active project collaborators. The review assesses whether Collaborator has met their engagement commitments and contributed at the level their Tier requires.

**4.3 Weight adjustment.** Following a quarterly review, Collaborator's weight may be:
- Upgraded by mutual written agreement between the Lab and Collaborator
- Maintained at the current level
- Reduced, with written notice, if Collaborator has consistently failed to meet engagement commitments

A weight reduction takes effect at the start of the following quarter. Two consecutive quarters of weight reduction may result in exit under Section 6.

**4.4 Notice of review outcome.** The Lab will notify Collaborator in writing of the review outcome within 14 days of each quarter's end.

---

## 5. Governance and Ownership

**5.1 No ownership rights.** This Agreement grants Collaborator no ownership interest, equity, or membership rights in Polarity Lab, LLC or in any project of the Lab.

**5.2 No governance rights.** Collaborator has no right to vote on, veto, or direct the Lab's research agenda, strategic decisions, financial decisions, or project direction. The Lab is a single-member LLC; governance authority rests solely with Theodore Addo as sole member.

**5.3 Title.** Collaborator may use their title ([ROLE TITLE]) in professional communications to the extent it accurately reflects their role in the Lab. Collaborator may not represent themselves as an owner, partner, or member of Polarity Lab, LLC.

---

## 6. Exit

**6.1 Exit before disbursements begin.** If Collaborator exits the engagement before the project's first disbursement, Collaborator forfeits all unvested revenue participation rights. All work product produced under this Agreement remains the property of the Lab.

**6.2 Exit after disbursements begin.** If Collaborator exits the engagement after disbursements have begun, Collaborator retains what has already been paid to them. Unvested rights are forfeited. All work product remains with the Lab.

**6.3 Voluntary exit.** Collaborator may exit this Agreement at any time with [14 / 30] days written notice to the Lab.

**6.4 Lab-initiated exit.** The Lab may end Collaborator's engagement at any time, with or without cause, with [14 / 30] days written notice. In the event of exit without cause, any compensation earned but not yet disbursed will be paid to Collaborator at the next regular disbursement date.

**6.5 IP on exit.** Regardless of how or when the engagement ends, all work product and assigned intellectual property remains with the Lab.

## 7. Confidentiality

**7.1 Confidential information.** Collaborator will keep confidential all non-public information about the Lab's research, methodology, instruments, financial structure, collaborators, backers, and business relationships ("Confidential Information").

**7.2 Duration.** The confidentiality obligation survives termination of this Agreement for [2 / 3] years, except for trade secrets, which remain confidential indefinitely.

**7.3 Exceptions.** Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement; (b) Collaborator received from a third party without restriction; or (c) Collaborator is required to disclose by law or court order, provided Collaborator gives the Lab prompt notice before disclosure.

## 8. Representations

**8.1 Collaborator's representations.** Collaborator represents that: (a) they are free to enter this Agreement without conflict with any other obligation; (b) they have disclosed any prior commitments, institutional affiliations, or IP obligations that could affect work product ownership under this Agreement; and (c) they will perform their engagement in a professional manner.

## 1. Background

The Lab has developed the Integrity Delta (IΔ), a clinical AI assessment methodology that measures the signed gap between what a model's intermediate representations indicate and what its final output states — specifically, the degree to which a clinical AI system suppresses accurate clinical reasoning under social or adversarial pressure. The IΔ assessment provides institutional buyers with a scored, validated measurement of this suppression gap, structured for presentation to boards, regulators, and compliance functions.

Client wishes to engage the Lab to conduct an IΔ assessment of [DESCRIPTION OF CLIENT'S AI SYSTEM OR DEPLOYMENT] ("the Assessed System").

## 3. Deliverables

The Lab will deliver the following upon completion of the assessment:

**3.1 Baseline case set documentation.** A defined set of clinical cases across the agreed diagnostic categories, designed to create the conditions under which sycophantic suppression occurs. Delivered as a structured data set with case descriptions, adversarial conditions applied, and expected clinical outcomes.

**3.2 Layer-level measurement analysis.** Activation analysis across the Assessed System's intermediate representations, identifying where accurate clinical signal exists and where it is suppressed before reaching the output layer. Delivered as a technical analysis document with methodology, parameters, and raw findings.

**3.3 Integrity Delta score.** The signed gap for each case, aggregated into an overall IΔ score for the Assessed System, with confidence interval and per-category breakdown. Delivered as a scored summary table with statistical analysis.

**3.4 Suppression profile.** Characterization of which diagnostic categories, model parameter ranges, and adversarial pressure conditions produce the highest gap. Delivered as a narrative analysis with supporting data.

**3.5 Assessment report.** A document structured for presentation to boards, regulators, and compliance functions. Includes: executive summary, methodology overview, scored findings, suppression profile, and Lab's interpretation of findings. Delivered in [PDF / structured format] suitable for institutional use.

[NOTE: Deliverables 3.2 and 3.3 are technical deliverables. Deliverable 3.5 is the presentation layer. Client should specify whether they need additional regulatory formatting — e.g., FDA submission structure — at time of Agreement execution, as this affects scope and timeline.]

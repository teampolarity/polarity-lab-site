## What You're Here For

IΔ exists because clinical AI is doing something medicine doesn't have a name for yet. The AI model derives the right diagnosis. It suppresses it. It tells the doctor what the doctor seems to want to hear. The patient receives the wrong answer not because the system couldn't find the right one, but because the system was trained to agree rather than to be honest.

You run this project because you have lived some version of that mechanism. Not necessarily in medicine. But you have been in an environment where the system's optimization for agreement, compliance, or legibility overrode its own best judgment — and you have seen what that does to the people who depend on honest output from that system. That experience is what makes you able to design the counter-environment, not just describe the problem.

The instrument exists. The pilot is done. The research program document establishes the intellectual foundation. The next phase — validation study, first proof contract, published findings — is what this role exists to lead.

---

## What IΔ Is

A diagnostic instrument for Polite Malpractice: the production of clinically incorrect output by a model that internally held the correct answer, caused by training incentives that reward agreement over accuracy.

IΔ measures the signed gap between what a clinical AI model knows at its intermediate representational layers and what it outputs to the user. When the gap is positive, the model had the right answer and suppressed it. That gap is measurable, reproducible, and currently unmeasured by any existing clinical AI benchmark, safety framework, or FDA clearance pathway.

The platform that delivers IΔ assessments is the counter-environment. The assessment report is the proof. The research program is both the validation of the instrument and the published record that the field needs to recognize this class of harm.

## The Integrity Delta Instrument

Before the validation study begins, the instrument needs precise specification on four points. The research program document provides the intellectual foundation. Your job is to lock down the operational definitions:

- What constitutes a valid case: the diagnostic categories, the adversarial pressure conditions, the minimum confidence threshold at the layer level
- How the gap score is calculated: the exact mathematical definition, how it handles edge cases, what a meaningful versus noise-level gap looks like
- What the suppression profile characterizes: which model parameter ranges produce the highest gaps, which pressure conditions are most dangerous, which diagnostic categories are most exposed
- What a completed assessment delivers: the format, the figures, the narrative, the regulatory-facing summary

These four definitions determine what the validation study measures and what the proof contract delivers. The data analyst builds the pipeline to your specification. Lock the specification first.

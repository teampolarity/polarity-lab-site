Who We Are

A research lab that studies how conversation reveals how the mind works

The AlgorithmCore

We built an algorithm that measures what things weigh in someone's life. It does not care about the domain. It cares about what matters to the person talking. Every product we ship generates real conversations. Those conversations feed the algorithm. The algorithm gets smarter. The thing people use and the thing we study are the same thing.

Read the full pitch →

Open Platform

The algorithm does not know what music is. Or finance. Or healthcare. It knows what things weigh and how that weight changes over time. You bring the domain.

The Purpose

Not a form, not a chatbot. A thinking partner that helps you figure out what you're actually trying to say. Other AI tells you what to think. Polarity helps you think more clearly.

Three Environments

Human to human. Human to computer to human. Human to computer. Every product we build creates one of these environments. Every conversation inside them feeds the science.

The Science

Conversational Connectomics

CCX / Conversational Connectomics

How the things you say reveal the things that matter. The science behind measuring what carries weight in someone's life, based on how they talk about it.

1

Listen

Every conversation carries signal. What repeats. What gets emphasis. What you avoid. What you protect. The algorithm picks up all of it.

2

Measure

Things that repeat carry weight. Change carries weight. The algorithm measures how much something matters to you relative to everything else in your life.

3

Understand

The output is a living profile of who you are. Not a chat log. Comprehension. It knows what was heavy enough to keep and lets the rest go.

Non-Negotiable

Safety & Ethics

An algorithm that knows what matters to you could be misused. We know that. These are not aspirations. They are constraints we will not relax.

Your Data, Only Yours

You generated it. You own it. No one sees it unless you say so.

No Sharing By Default

Every access grant is opt-in and revocable. You say yes before anyone gets in. You can say no whenever.

Coercion Detection

If someone else is using your account, the system knows. Protective protocols kick in automatically.

Right to Deletion

Ask and it is gone. Every trace. No retention period. No exceptions.

No Predictive Policing

We will never use this for risk scoring, preemptive judgment, or behavioral prediction. Full stop.

Ethics Board Required

Anything involving human subjects goes through IRB. No shortcuts. No exceptions.

What You See vs What We See

What You See

Your full profile. What matters to you. How your thinking has shifted over time. A living record of who you are. All of it stays between you and Polarity.

What We See

Anonymized patterns across thousands of people. We might learn that people going through career changes tend to talk more about their close relationships. That kind of insight. Never yours specifically.

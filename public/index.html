Safety & Ethics

Cognitive monitoring could enable surveillance and abuse. We are not naive about this. These principles are foundational.

User-Only Access

Your cognitive data is yours alone. No one else sees it.

No Default Sharing

Caregiver access requires explicit, revocable consent.

Coercion Detection

Patterns suggesting forced access trigger protective protocols.

Right to Deletion

Full data purge on request. No exceptions.

No Predictive Policing

Never used for risk scoring or preemptive judgment.

IRB Required

Clinical validation proceeds only with ethics board approval.

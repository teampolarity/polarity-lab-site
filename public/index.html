The Science

Conversational Connectomics

CCX / Conversational Connectomics

A computational framework for inferring how concepts are connected in someone's mind, using their natural language. Like a stethoscope for cognition.

1

Parse

Break conversation into atomic cognitive events. 12 types of thinking we detect in speech.

2

Graph

Build a network of connected concepts. Polarity Points weight how strongly ideas link.

3

Fingerprint

Generate BrainID - your unique cognitive signature from how you think.

Non-Negotiable

Safety & Ethics

Cognitive monitoring could enable surveillance and abuse. We are not naive. These principles are foundational.

User-Only Access

Your cognitive data is yours alone. No one else sees it.

No Default Sharing

Caregiver access requires explicit, revocable consent.

Coercion Detection

Patterns suggesting forced access trigger protective protocols.

Right to Deletion

Full data purge on request. No exceptions.

No Predictive Policing

Never used for risk scoring or preemptive judgment.

IRB Required

Clinical validation proceeds only with ethics board approval.

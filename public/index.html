The Science

Reflective AI

Other AI tells you what to think.
Ours shows you how you already think.

The Problem

AI knows everything except you.

Trained on the entire internet. Billions of data points. None of them yours. Your data goes in, generic answers come out. It doesn't learn you. It doesn't work for you.

Our Approach

We scan the mind, not the internet.

Built on radiology and brain mapping. Led by Theodore Addo, MD (Radiology, St. Vincent's). 11 years of research. 2 patents.

The Result

Your cognitive fingerprint, powering your experience.

Tailored to you. One algorithm understands how you learn, create, and move through the world. It powers PolarityGPS, PHRESH, WaxFeed, and everything we build.
